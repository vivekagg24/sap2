Blind Stock Count

Blind Stock Count Help.

Enter a Plant and Storage Location on the first screen. This is retained throughout the count

Scan or enter the material number and quantity counted. Press "Add item" to add material to the count sheet.

A maximum of 25 items can be counted for each Physical inventory document posting

Once the items to be counted have been added press "Post Count" and confirm. Enter serial numbers if required and press "POST"

You will prompted if any variances exist and can then correct these on the variance tab

Press "Confirm Post" to complete the count
